Prerequisites

  • Basic notions in differential geometry, complex/functional analysis (Tangent space, Cauchy integral, Fourier series, ...).
  • First course in Lie group/algebra and representation (PBW basis, Verma modules, induced reps, triangular decomposition, ...).
  • Basics on cohomology and central extension of Lie algebras (Reviewed in [S] Chapters 3 and 4).
  • No physics background will be assumed, though it will be helpful.

  • Pavel Mnev, Lecture notes on Conformal Field Theory
  • Vertex Operator Algebra: Victor Kac, Vertex Algebras for Beginners [K]
  • Frenkel & Ben-Zvi, Vertex Algebras and Algebraic Curves
  • J. Lepowsky & H. Li, Introduction to Vertex Operator Algebras and Their Representations
